Primary

"The Beatles Interviews Database." Beatles Ultimate Experience: Britannica, n.d. Web. 14 Sept. 2014.

"Beatles: First Radio Interview 10/28/1962 - Beatles Interviews Database." Britannica, n.d. Web. 14 Sept. 2014.

We chose this source as its documented as being one of the Beatles’s first radio interviews. At this time Ringo Starr was still a new member to the group.


"John Lennon Interview: Rolling Stone Magazine, 12/5/1980 - Beatles Interviews Database." Britannica, n.d. Web. 14 Sept. 2014.

We chose this source because it’s the last interview given by John Lennon before his death in 1980, where he was shot on the streets of New York
